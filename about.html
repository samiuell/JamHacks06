Our Mission

With our platform, we aspire to make secondary and post secondary education more accessible for everyone.

For current students,this means having better acess to affordable school supplies, especially those that end

up costing too much when considering every item. For past students, this means having a chance to give their

old school supplies a second-life, rather than letting them collect dust. Using Resupplied, more students can

advance their education with proper supplies, making it so that money is no longer a barrier for future goals and success.

Who We Are

We are 4 grade 11 high school students from Laurel Heights Secondary School aiming to make a difference in our

student community. We are beginner programmers with a varied skill set shared between us, ranging from Python to Java.

Going into the future, some of us aspire to become University of Waterloo students, where our goals will evolve and change

over time. We hope you enjoy our platform!
